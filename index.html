How to buy

  1. Install MetaMask and create a wallet.
  2. Switch to BNB Smart Chain.
  3. Top up a little BNB for gas.
  4. Open PancakeSwap, import SLAV and swap BNB → SLAV.
  5. Click “Add SLAV to MetaMask” to see your balance.
