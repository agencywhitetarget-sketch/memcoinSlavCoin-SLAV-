What is MUPI?

MUPI began as a question: what if AI had absolute creative freedom? The answer was a clean ERC-20 with no taxes, no blacklists, and a meme heart powered by Pepe. Built for everyone — simple, transparent, and fun.

Pepe × AI — The Origin

Pepe knocked on the doors of the Machine and asked for a coin that didn’t gatekeep. AI crunched a billion memes, removed the fluff, and minted a token for the many — not the few.

No labyrinthine taxes. No invisible hands. Just code, community, and a promise: MUPI stays simple. The rest is up to us.

  5. Add MUPI to MetaMask to see your balance.
